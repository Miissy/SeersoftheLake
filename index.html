An FC, a Family

We're a tightknit community in the Mist district of Twintania. Here in SOTL, we pride ourselves in being welcoming, considerate, kind, and open-minded to all people of different walks of life.

Rules

Conduct

Most important rule, be kind, welcoming, considerate. Do not be passive aggressive, rude, or salty. This is a safe place. We will not tolerate bullying of any kind regardless of Free Company rank, length of service or position. In an event where we feel this rule has been broken, a member will be muted and taken to a private room for discussions; where a warning or ban may be applied.


Disclaimer

The leadership team reserve the right to remove/ban/mute users without warning or notice or when the user is found to be purposely trying to push leaders boundaries or test our limits. Disrespecting us or our rules, or consistently "walking the line" for your own amusement will not be tolerated


Beginner-friendly Policy

We offer aid to beginners and we do not judge others according to their game preferences. If you're in need of help or advice regarding gameplay, we're here to help when we're able to. We want to create a safe environment for people who are curious and willing to learn. We do NOT tolerate any form of elitism - we all play this game for fun and toxicity has no place here.
